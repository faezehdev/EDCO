Edco Makes your life brigher

Every company has its own story and this is our story.

In 1379, our company started importing some electrical goods and started shipping as a general trading company in the Middle East region, but soon the young owners of the company changed the form of the company to a manufacturing company.
And now we are a manufacturer of German design switches and sockets with a quality that can be compared to most famous and well-known high quality companies like Schneider, Berker, Hager, etc.
And now after almost 22 years, from classic to modern design switches and sockets, with more than 200 types of products and even beyond, we are going to higher levels of quality and technology-based products.
